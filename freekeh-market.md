<p><p>Freekeh is a nutritious ancient grain, available primarily in two market types: wholegrain and cracked. Wholegrain freekeh consists of unprocessed green durum wheat, retaining its natural nutrients, fiber, and flavor, making it ideal for salads, pilafs, and side dishes. Cracked freekeh, on the other hand, is processed for quicker cooking, featuring smaller, broken pieces. It is versatile and often used in soups, stews, and grain bowls. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking plant-based, high-protein food options.</p></p>
