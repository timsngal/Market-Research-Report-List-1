<p><p>Polyisocyanurate Foam (PIR) Sandwich Panels are composite materials consisting of a core of PIR foam sandwiched between two facings, typically made from metal, plywood, or other materials. These panels are renowned for their high thermal insulation properties, fire resistance, and structural integrity, making them ideal for various applications, including commercial buildings, cold storage, and roofing systems.</p><p>The market for PIR sandwich panels is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient construction materials and the rising emphasis on sustainable building practices.
