<p><p>In the data center market, server power supplies are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Open Frame Power Supply**: These units lack an enclosing chassis, allowing for better airflow and cooling, making them ideal for high-density setups. </p><p>2. **Single Power Supply**: A straightforward option providing power to the server. These are less complex but can pose risks if the unit fails.</p><p>3. **Redundant Power Supply**: Designed for reliability, these systems use multiple power sources to ensure uninterrupted operation, minimizing downtime in critical environments.</p></p>
