<p><p>The medical PVC adhesive tapes market is divided into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided tapes have adhesive on one side, making them ideal for securing dressings or devices to the skin. They are easy to apply and provide reliable adhesion. Double-sided tapes feature adhesive on both sides, facilitating bond attachment between two surfaces, often used in wound care or prosthetic applications. Both types cater to the healthcare industry's need for secure, effective, and skin-friendly adhesion solutions.</p></p>
