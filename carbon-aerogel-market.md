<p><p>The carbon aerogel market is categorized into granules, powder, and other forms. Granules are small, irregularly shaped beads that enhance ease of handling and application, making them ideal for composite materials and insulation. Powdered carbon aerogel offers a finer consistency, suitable for applications requiring high surface area and reactivity, such as in catalysis and filtration. Other forms may include monoliths or films, each tailored for specialized uses in electronics, energy storage, and environmental remediation, showcasing the versatility of carbon aerogels.</p></p>
