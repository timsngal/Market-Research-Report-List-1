<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Miniature Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Reflective Sensor</li><li>Retroreflective Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Miniature Photoelectric Sensor market encompasses three main types: Through-Beam Sensors, Diffuse Reflective Sensors, and Retroreflective Sensors. Through-Beam Sensors consist of a separate transmitter and receiver, detecting objects based on interruption of light beams. Diffuse Reflective Sensors have both transmitter and receiver in one unit, detecting objects by reflecting emitted light off surfaces. Retroreflective Sensors also use a single unit but rely on a retroreflective target to bounce back the light to the receiver, enhancing detection reliability and range.</p></p>
