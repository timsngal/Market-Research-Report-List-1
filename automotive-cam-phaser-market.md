<p><ul><li>Vane-type Cam Phaser</li><li>Star Shaped Cam Phaser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cam phaser market consists primarily of two types: vane-type cam phasers and star-shaped cam phasers. Vane-type cam phasers use internal rotating vanes to adjust the timing of the camshaft, enhancing engine performance and efficiency. They are commonly employed in modern variable valve timing systems. In contrast, star-shaped cam phasers utilize a star-shaped design to alter the camshaft position, offering simpler configurations and robust performance. Both types contribute to improved engine dynamics, fuel efficiency, and reduced emissions.</p></p>
