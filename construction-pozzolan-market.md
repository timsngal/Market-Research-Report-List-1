<p><p>The construction pozzolan market is divided into two primary types: artificial and natural pozzolans. Artificial pozzolans are man-made materials, such as fly ash, silica fume, and slag, which are produced from industrial processes and enhance concrete properties. Natural pozzolans, derived from volcanic ash, clay, and diatomaceous earth, occur naturally and possess pozzolanic properties, improving durability and strength. Both types are crucial in sustainable construction, reducing environmental impact while enhancing the performance of concrete mixtures.</p></p>
