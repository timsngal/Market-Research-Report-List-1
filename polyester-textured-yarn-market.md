<p><p>The polyester textured yarn market is categorized based on the denier (D) measurement, which indicates the yarn's thickness and weight. Common types include 50D, 75D, 100D, 150D, 200D, and 300D. Lower deniers like 50D and 75D are lighter and often used for delicate fabrics, while higher deniers such as 150D, 200D, and 300D provide more durability and strength, suitable for heavier applications like upholstery. The "Other" category includes specialized or custom denier products tailored for specific needs.</p></p>
