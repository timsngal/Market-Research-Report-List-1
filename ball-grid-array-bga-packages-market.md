<p><p>Ball Grid Array (BGA) packages are a type of surface-mount technology used in electronic devices that facilitate the interconnection of semiconductor chips with circuit boards. This design enhances electrical performance and reliability while minimizing the footprint of the component.</p><p>The Ball Grid Array (BGA) Packages Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in sectors such as consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications.
