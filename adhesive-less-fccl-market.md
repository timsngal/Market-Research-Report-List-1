<p><p>The adhesive-less flexible copper clad laminate (FCCL) market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided FCCL features a copper layer on one side, ideal for applications where wiring is required on a single surface, such as basic circuits. In contrast, double-sided FCCL incorporates copper layers on both sides, allowing for more complex circuit designs and improved performance in advanced electronics. Both types cater to different requirements in the rapidly evolving electronics industry, emphasizing efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
