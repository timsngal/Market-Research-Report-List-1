<p><p>The Power Pole market comprises four main types: reinforced concrete, steel, wood, and composite. Reinforced concrete poles offer durability and high strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Steel poles provide excellent resistance to environmental stress but may require corrosion protection. Wood poles, traditionally used, are cost-effective but have a shorter lifespan and vulnerability to pests. Composite poles combine materials for enhanced strength, corrosion resistance, and reduced weight, making them increasingly popular in modern infrastructure projects.</p></p>
