<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Torque Only Sensors (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensors (TAS). TOS measures the steering wheel's rotational force, providing essential feedback for optimal steering assistance. In contrast, TAS captures both the torque and the angle of the steering wheel, enhancing precision in control. Each sensor type plays a crucial role in improving vehicle handling and safety, catering to the growing demand for advanced steering technologies in modern vehicles.</p></p>
